What we do

One local renderer for the whole job.

New builds, renovations, repairs and feature walls — inside and out. If it needs rendering, Rainbow Renderers can handle it.

Acrylic rendering

Flexible, coloured-through acrylic render for a clean, modern look that resists cracking and fading.

Cement & sand render

Traditional, tough sand-and-cement render — the hard-wearing base that lasts on brick and block.

New builds & feature walls

Whole-home render on new builds and extensions, plus standout feature and fence walls.
