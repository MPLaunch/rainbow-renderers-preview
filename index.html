What we do

One local renderer for the whole job.

New builds, renovations, repairs and feature walls — inside and out. If it needs rendering, Rainbow Renderers handles it start to finish.

Acrylic rendering

Flexible, coloured-through acrylic render for a clean, modern finish that resists cracking and fading.

Cement & sand render

Traditional, tough sand-and-cement render — the hard-wearing base that lasts on brick and block.
